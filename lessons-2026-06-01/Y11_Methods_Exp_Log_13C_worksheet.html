Key results — $y=a^x$ ($a>0,\;a\ne 1$)

Domain $\mathbb{R}$ · Range $\mathbb{R}^+$ · $y$-int $(0,1)$ · Asymptote $y=0$

Vertical shift by $c$ moves the asymptote to $y=c$. A leading minus reflects in the $x$-axis.
