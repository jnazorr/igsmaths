Challenge

Q6. Right triangle, $40^\circ$ angle, opposite side $7$ cm:

a $\sin 40^\circ=\dfrac{7}{h}\;\Rightarrow\;h=\dfrac{7}{\sin 40^\circ}\approx$ $10.89$ cm (hypotenuse)
b $\tan 40^\circ=\dfrac{7}{a}\;\Rightarrow\;a=\dfrac{7}{\tan 40^\circ}\approx$ $8.34$ cm (adjacent)
