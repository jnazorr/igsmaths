Q6. $x$-intercepts $(3,0)$, $(7,0)$, turning point $(5,12)$, form $y=m(x-3)(x-7)$:

a $x=5$ it is halfway between the intercepts: $\tfrac{3+7}{2}=5$.
b $m=-3$ $12=m(5-3)(5-7)=m(2)(-2)=-4m \Rightarrow m=-3$.
c $y=-3(x-3)(x-7)$ (opens downward, so $(5,12)$ is a maximum).
