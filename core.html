Exam format reminders

Year 10 Core Semester 1 exams

  • Exam 1: 60 minutes writing time · NO calculator · 40 marks · 12 short-answer questions.
  • Exam 2: 90 minutes writing time · CAS allowed · 60 marks · 20 multiple-choice + 4 extended-response.
  • Both exams include 15 minutes reading time and a supplied formula sheet.
  • Standard writing equipment in a transparent case · no phones, watches, smart devices, food, bags, headphones, or electronic dictionaries.
