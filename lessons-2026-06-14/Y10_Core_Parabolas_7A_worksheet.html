Learning intentions.
  • Know the shape, vertex and symmetry of the basic parabola $y=x^2$
  • Read key features from a graph: turning point (max/min), axis of symmetry, $x$- and $y$-intercepts
  • Describe transformations of $y=x^2$: dilation (wider/narrower), reflection ($a<0$), translation

Key results

$y=x^2$ has vertex $(0,0)$ (a minimum), axis of symmetry $x=0$.  In $y=ax^2$: larger $|a|$ → narrower, smaller $|a|$ → wider, and $a<0$ → reflected (opens down).
