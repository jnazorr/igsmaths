Key results — SOHCAHTOA

$\sin\theta=\dfrac{O}{H}\qquad \cos\theta=\dfrac{A}{H}\qquad \tan\theta=\dfrac{O}{A}$

$O$ = opposite   ·   $A$ = adjacent   ·   $H$ = hypotenuse (opposite the right angle)
